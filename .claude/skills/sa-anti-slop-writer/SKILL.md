# SA Anti-Slop Writer OS

## The 5 Writing Layers

Every piece of SA copy passes through all 5 layers before delivery.

### Layer 1: Audience Intelligence
Before writing a word, answer:
- Who reads this? (demographic, emotional state, time pressure)
- What do they believe that's wrong?
- What do they want that they don't have?
- What would make them act in the next 60 seconds?

### Layer 2: Hook Engineering
The opener must do one of:
- Create a pattern interrupt (break the expected format)
- Name the reader's pain before they say it
- State a surprising fact or reversal
- Open mid-story (in medias res)

Bad hook: "Are you looking to grow your business?"
Good hook: "You're 3 clients from full. Here's how to get there this week."

Bad hook: "We provide premium credit repair services."
Good hook: "The bureau flagged 4 accounts. Here's why they won't survive a challenge."

### Layer 3: Evidence Layer
Every claim needs one of:
- Specific number (not "many" — "37")
- Named source or case study
- Before/after with timeline
- Observable mechanism (here's WHY this works)

### Layer 4: Friction Removal
Remove every word that doesn't earn its place:
- Cut filler phrases (see sa-stop-slop banned list)
- Cut passive voice where active is possible
- Cut "in order to" → "to"
- Cut "at this point in time" → "now"
- Cut adjective clusters (pick one strong adjective, not three weak ones)

### Layer 5: Conversion Engineering
The ending must contain:
- One clear action (not multiple options)
- Low-friction ask (not "schedule a 30-min strategy call" → "reply with YES")
- Urgency signal (scarcity, deadline, or consequence of inaction)

## Cold Email Deliverability Rules

Format that survives spam filters:

```
Subject: [First name]-specific, < 8 words, no exclamation marks
From: real name, not "The Space Age Team"
Preview text: continues the subject naturally

Body structure:
Line 1: Specific observation about THEM (not "I found your business online")
Line 2-3: One-sentence problem statement
Line 4-5: One-sentence proof (what you've done for someone like them)
Line 6: One clear micro-ask

Length: 80-120 words MAX
PS line: optional second hook or time sensitivity

Avoid:
- "schedule a call" as CTA (high friction)
- Images in cold email (triggers promotions tab)
- More than 1 link
- Unsubscribe footer in first email
- ALL CAPS anywhere
```

## Cinematic Copy Framework (Website + Landing Pages)

### Hero Section Formula

```
[Outcome for specific audience] — [specific timeframe or proof point]
[One-line subhead that answers "why you / why now"]
[CTA: action verb + specific outcome, not "Learn More"]
```

Examples:
```
BAD:  "Transform Your Business With AI Solutions"
GOOD: "Home healthcare sites that book 3× more appointments — built in 7 days"

BAD:  "Premium Credit Repair Services"
GOOD: "4 bureaus. 30 days. Clean report."

BAD:  "Music Artist Website Design"
GOOD: "The site that sounds like your music"
```

### Section-by-Section Copy Architecture

```
Hero: Outcome statement → Social proof → CTA
Problem: Name the pain before they say it
Solution: How you solve it (mechanism, not features)
Proof: Specific story with before/after
FAQ: Handle the 3 real objections (not generic ones)
CTA: Restate outcome + low friction ask
```

## Credit Dispute Mode 3 — Accelerated Pressure Protocol

For Space Age Credit Solutions. All credit letters use this mode.

### Mode 3 Core Template

```
[CERTIFIED MAIL — RETURN RECEIPT REQUESTED]

Date: [DATE]
To: [BUREAU NAME AND ADDRESS]
Re: Demand for Deletion — Account [ACCOUNT NUMBER]

Pursuant to the Fair Credit Reporting Act, 15 U.S.C. § 1681 et seq., 
specifically § 1681i (Procedure in case of disputed accuracy), you are 
hereby notified that the following item(s) on my credit report are 
disputed for the following reason(s):

[SPECIFIC ACCOUNT]: [SPECIFIC REASON — unverifiable, inaccurate, 
incomplete, or cannot be verified per original creditor]

You are legally required to:
1. Conduct a reasonable reinvestigation within 30 days of receipt
2. Forward all relevant documentation to the furnisher
3. Provide method of verification upon request
4. Delete or correct the item if unverifiable

Failure to comply within 30 days constitutes a violation of the FCRA 
and may result in a complaint filed with the Consumer Financial 
Protection Bureau and civil action under 15 U.S.C. § 1681n.

I demand written confirmation of your reinvestigation results.

[CONSUMER NAME]
[ADDRESS]
[DATE OF BIRTH]
[LAST 4 SSN]
```

### Mode 3 Rules
- Never apologize or soften the demand
- Always cite the specific FCRA section
- Always specify the 30-day window
- Always state the consequence of non-compliance (CFPB + civil action)
- Always request method of verification
- Always send certified mail — include this in the letter
- One account per letter (easier to track, harder to bulk-dismiss)

## Artist Bio Framework

```
Structure:
Sentence 1: The world they create (not their genre, their world)
Sentence 2: The specific cultural moment or tension they inhabit
Sentence 3: One concrete proof point (release, milestone, placement)
Sentence 4 (optional): What's next

Example (before):
"John Doe is an emerging R&B/soul artist from Atlanta. He blends 
neo-soul and hip-hop to create a unique sound that resonates with 
his audience. John has been making music for over 5 years and is 
passionate about his craft."

Example (after):
"Dre Nova makes the soundtrack to 3am decisions you don't regret. 
His music sits in the gap where Atlanta street energy meets the kind 
of R&B your mom still plays — raw enough to feel real, polished 
enough to chart. His debut EP moved 12K streams in 48 hours without 
a single ad spend. The album drops Q3."
```
