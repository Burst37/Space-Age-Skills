Our Story

Born From a Storm.
Built for Connection.

“After Katrina, I watched communities rebuild — not through apps, but through people simply showing up for each other.”

Hurricane Katrina didn’t just destroy buildings — it scattered communities. Watching people find strength through shared experience planted a seed that 15 years of building technology would grow into Fun on the Run.

The idea is simple but rare: what if an app got people off their phones and into the same room? Not to follow each other — but to do things together.

2005
Hurricane Katrina — the moment that changed everything
2005 – 2020
15 years in tech, watching social media isolate instead of connect
2020
The pandemic made the loneliness crisis impossible to ignore — FOTR was born
Now
Beta launching — be among the first to redefine how you connect
